About

Dr. Monika Aggarwal was born in Dehra Dun, India. She received the B.Tech. degree in Electrical Engineering and the M.Tech. degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the Regional Engineering College, Kurukshetra, India, and the Ph. D. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi, India, in 1993, 1995, and 2000, respectively. She was employed with Hughes Software Systems (HSS), Gurgaon, India from 1999 to 2002. During 2001 she was a visiting researcher in the Dept. of Systems and Control, Uppsala University, Uppsala, Sweden. She joined C.A.R.E, I.I.T Delhi as Asst.Professor in Jan.2003.

Research Interest

Array Signal Processing

  • Developed several novel Maximum Likelihood, High Resolution algorithms for direction of arrival estimation and beam-forming mainly for wideband sources, spread sources and wideband spread sources.

  • Developed simple, elegant and robust algorithms for direction of arrival estimation.

  • Studied array design problems, inherent Array Ambiguities present due to the array geometry.

  • Developing a system of single Acoustic vector sensor to estimate the direction of arrival and beam-forming.

  • Successfully Tested the developed underwater acoustic modem in Bay of Bengal.

  • Developing the Visible light based Underwater communication module to enhance data rate

Communication

  • Implemented G3-FAX modems (V.21, V.27ter, V.29 and V.17).

  • Designing linear pre-filter, space-time codes to improve the performance of communication systems.

  • Developing computationally simple methods to implement communication systems.

  • Developed acoustic underwater communication acoustic modem, combating the effects of sever multi-path, Doppler etc.

  • Used to approach underwater communications problems from the perspective of Time Reversal Mirrors.

  • Successfully Tested the developed underwater acoustic modem in Bay of Bengal.

  • Developing the Visible light based Underwater communication module to enhance data rate


Underwater Acoustics

  • Realizing a low power acoustic signal processing module for remote surveillance manned/unmanned platform.

  • Designing and implementation of robust hybrid detector for detection of underwater passive surveillance.

  • Designed a target classifier for passive surveillance sonar and passive sonar for multi sensor data fusion.

    • Technologies transferred in 2006.

    • Implemented at CIAO system.

  • Designed optimum methods of signal enhancement, spectrum estimation and identification of coupled machineries for passive sonar.

    • To be implemented at UWR, GOA in May’06.

Medical Signal/Image Processing

  • Designing and developing automatic early detection techniques for Glaucoma and DR using Fundus images.

  • Developing a robust ECG reader for better understanding of ECG signals

  • Astute Stethoscope: A smart 5G enabled stethoscope for understanding the lungs.

  • EEG Inverse problem solution is being developed using concept of underdetermined source localization formulation


5G-IOT

  • Designing and developing 5G NB-IOT based module with plug and play capabilities

  • Developed AQI system with all hazardous gas and measurement
